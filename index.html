Who are we?

We are a community of Rajadhiraja yoga enthusiasts dedicated to spreading the timeless wisdom of yoga. Each week, we hold in-person, donation-based classes, keeping them essentially free to ensure maximum accessibility for everyone. Our sessions include dynamic and static meditations, asanas (yoga postures), yogic dances, and songs, as well as Satsang (spiritual discussions) and lectures. We warmly invite you to join us on this unending journey of self-discovery, growth, and global neo-humanistic elevation—together, we’re shaping a more conscious tomorrow. At the heart of our practice is a deep belief in nurturing all three facets of human life: the physical, the mental, and the spiritual. Come experience it for yourself!
